Idioms Semantic Matching

In the spirit of LangNote, we build another feature that helps you learn English phrases and idioms more effectively. Given an (highlighted) English sentence, our model suggests 3-5 expressions that are most related to the meaning of the sentence. Here we present 30 output samples of our demo version. This is obviously imperfect, but we will improve it further. Give it a try first and let us know if you find it useful.
